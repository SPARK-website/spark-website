About SPARK

SPARK stands for Safety, Protection, Awareness, Rescue, and Knowledge. This website is your go-to guide for essential safety knowledge, covering everything from first aid to cyber security and mental wellness. Our goal is to empower teens and young adults with the information they need to stay safe and confident in any situation.

Disclaimer

This site only provides information about basic first aid and safety. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you have any questions regarding a medical health condition, always seek out a qualified health provider. In an emergency, call your local emergency number immediately.
