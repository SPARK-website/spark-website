RESOURCES AND HELP

Trusted helplines and emergency numbers:

  • United Nations Women provides information and resources on women's safety.
  • The International Committee of the Red Cross (ICRC) offers support and resources for survivors of sexual violence.
  • Child Helpline International assists children and provides aid for over 3 million children and young people.
  • Make sure that you know the emergency number for your country or region.

How to find support if you feel unsafe or need help:

  • Go to generally trustworthy adults like teachers or security staff.
  • If you feel unsafe, look for women with children, as they are more likely to help in these kinds of situations.
  • Always stay in populated areas.
  • Use your phone and call/text a trusted person immediately.
  • Always trust your instincts.

Disclaimer:

This information is for general awareness only. Emergency numbers and available resources can vary by country and region. Always verify and know the correct emergency numbers for your specific location. In an emergency, your first action should be to contact your local emergency services.
